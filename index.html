🔍 The Problem — What We Found in Our Survey
🚪

No point of contact

Trainees don't know who to reach out to or how to get introduced to the field

🗺

Don't know how to start

Interest exists, but there's no clear first step or roadmap to follow

🧰

Lack of skills & resources

Limited access to research training, tools, and learning materials

RECAP’s Solution ▼
💡 What RECAP Is Building
1
🤝

Specialty Collaboratives

Groups of experienced members ready to guide trainees in their specialty of interest

2
📅

Monthly Skill Sessions

Regular meetings to learn research skills and practice presenting research

3
📚

Resource Repository

A shared library of research tools, templates, and reference materials

✅ Expected Outcomes
📈

Trainees gain real skills

Leading to meaningful, high-quality research output

🥇

Mentors get recognition

Formal credit for guiding others — great for their academic portfolio

🌐

Stronger connections

A network of people united by specialty interest and shared purpose
